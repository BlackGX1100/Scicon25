Registration

Register now to secure your spot at SciCon 2025. Early bird rates available until April 11, 2025.

To register, fill out the form below and send your payment to 38-9014-0148381-00.

Put your name and school in the reference, e.g. John Smith Logan Park High School.

Full Registration:

You have access to the full conference over the entire duration and can participate in all associated evening activities, including the dinner.

One Day Registration:

You have access to the conference for one of the two days. Please note that evening activities, including the dinner, are not included in a one-day registration.

Extra for Dinner:

A full registration entitles you to one additional guest to the dinner for an extra fee. A one-day registration can purchase the dinner for themselves and an additional guest if there is enough space.

Prices:

One day - $250

Full experience if you register before 11th April 2025 - $490

Full experience if you register after 11th April 2025 - $550

Additional dinner fee - $120
